About Me

Emerging in 2019 from Oyo State, Nigeria, Mide blends Pop, Hip hop, Trap, Drill, R&B, soul, Experimental, Afrobeat, Dancehall, Rock, EDM, Hard rock, Folk, and Cinematic elements into a genre-defying sound.

Mide is a Nigerian music producer shaping the future of Afrobeat and Amapiano with his signature blend of bouncy percussion and smooth, melodic chords. Recognised by AfrobeatProducers.com and collaborators worldwide, he has worked with artists across the globe, helping them refine their sound, grow their brand identity, and expand their following. Known for blending heavy, energetic drums with smooth, melodic chords, he creates rhythms that strike a balance between groove and emotion. Inspired by leading producers such as Pprime, Blaisebeatz, and London, Mide brings a fresh perspective to Afrobeat, fusing traditional percussive bounce with modern sonic textures.

A versatile creative, Mide has also collaborated with rising producers in Nigeria, building a reputation for his ability to create grooves that connect instantly with listeners. While rooted in Afrobeat and Amapiano, hs openness to experimenting with other genres sets him apart as a forward-thinking producer with a global vision.

A multi-instrumentalist who plays the piano and drums, Mide draws on live elements to bring authenticity to his productions. His versatility allows him to move effortlessly between mid-tempo Afrobeat, fast-paced Afropiano, and even cross-genre experiments with rock and progressive metal. Whether building beats for artists or crafting his own unique sound, Mide's goal is simple: to make music that moves people.
